Dating Safety Tips

At Loop Social, we believe that meeting new people should be an exciting experience, but we also prioritize your safety above all else. Whether you're exchanging messages or planning to meet in person, it's essential to exercise caution and use your best judgment. Here are some guidelines to help you stay safe and secure throughout your Loop Social experience:

ONLINE SAFETY
1. Never Share Financial Information
  • Avoid sending money or sharing financial details, especially over wire transfer. If someone requests money from you, report it immediately.
2. Protect Your Personal Information
  • Refrain from sharing sensitive personal information such as your social security number, home or work address, or details about your daily routine with strangers.
3. Stay on the Platform
  • Keep your conversations within the Loop Social platform. Avoid moving the conversation to external messaging apps or platforms.
4. Be Wary of Long-Distance Relationships
  • Exercise caution with individuals who claim to be from your country but are currently overseas. Watch out for red flags such as avoiding in-person meetings or video calls.
5. Report Suspicious Behavior
  • If you encounter any suspicious or offensive behavior, promptly report it to us. This includes requests for money, harassment, fraudulent profiles, or any other violations of our community guidelines.

PROTECT YOUR ACCOUNT
1. Choose a Strong Password
  • Select a secure password for your account and avoid sharing your login credentials with anyone. Loop Social will never ask for your username and password via email.

MEETING IN PERSON
1. Take Your Time
  • Get to know the other person before agreeing to meet offline. Use messaging and video calls as screening tools to assess compatibility and ensure your safety.
2. Meet in Public Places
  • For initial meetings, choose populated, public locations. Avoid private settings such as homes or secluded areas.
3. Inform Someone
  • Always inform a friend or family member about your plans, including the location and time of your meeting. Keep your phone charged and with you at all times.
4. Transportation
  • Be in control of your transportation arrangements. If you're driving yourself, have a backup plan such as a ride-sharing app or a trusted friend available.
5. Set Boundaries
  • Know your limits regarding alcohol and drug consumption. Don't hesitate to end the date if you feel uncomfortable or pressured.
6. Stay Vigilant
  • Keep an eye on your drinks and personal belongings at all times. If you feel uneasy or unsafe, seek assistance from staff or leave the situation immediately.

LGBTQ+ TRAVEL
1. Exercise Caution
  • Be aware of the laws and potential risks when traveling to new places. Exercise extra caution in areas where LGBTQ+ rights may be limited or where dating app usage could pose risks.

SEXUAL HEALTH & CONSENT
1. Protect Yourself
  • Use protection such as condoms to reduce the risk of sexually transmitted infections (STIs). Stay informed about vaccination options for STIs such as HPV.
2. Know Your Status:
  • Regularly get tested for STIs to ensure your health and prevent their spread. Seek medical assistance if you experience any symptoms.
3. Communicate
  • Discuss sexual health and consent with your partner before engaging in physical intimacy. Respect each other's boundaries and be aware of the importance of ongoing communication.
4. Consent
  • Consent is crucial in all sexual interactions. Ensure that both parties are willing and able to consent, and respect each other's autonomy at all times.

At Loop Social, your safety and well-being are our top priorities. By following these guidelines and using common sense, we strive to create a secure and enjoyable environment for all our users. If you have any concerns or encounter any issues, please don't hesitate to reach out to our support team.
